Frequently Asked Questions

What is a smart society?

A smart society is a society that is interconnected and sustainable, through the use of technology.

Why are smart societies important?

As newer technology is developed and our societies progress, we need to ensure that this development is sustainable with minimal environment footprint.

Why are China and Japan collaborating?

We feel that both China and Japan have a long way to go in making their countries greener and more efficient, and with solutions from both countries, both countries would be able to benefit from this exchange.
